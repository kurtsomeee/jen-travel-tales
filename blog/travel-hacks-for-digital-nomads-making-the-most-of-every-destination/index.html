Behind Jen Travel Tales
Structure keeps me grounded. Travel keeps me growing.

Hi! I'm Jen, the creator behind Jen Travel Tales, where I document my journey as a digital nomad, content creator, and Top-Rated Plus freelancer living a work-from-anywhere lifestyle.

Based in the Philippines, I've been traveling through the vibrant cities and hidden gems of Japan, the UAE, Singapore, Taiwan, Vietnam, Hong Kong, Bangkok, Bali, Kuala Lumpur, Seoul, Shenzhen, and Macau, and I've only grown more intentional with every journey.

With a decade of experience in social media management, digital marketing, and virtual assistance, I've worked with clients from start-ups to mid-sized companies across diverse industries, helping them elevate their online presence, craft engaging content, and build streamlined, results-driven marketing strategies. All while building a life that blends meaningful work with my love for travel.

I believe you don't have to choose between building a career and seeing the world. With the right systems, structure, and mindset, you can thrive in both. Through Jen Travel Tales, I share my real experiences, travel diaries, and remote work tips to inspire others who dream of breaking free from the 9-5 and creating their own path.
